What is a Front-End Developer?

A Front-End Developer has the knowledge and ability to take a design or idea and turn it into a real, usable interface. They focus on building the parts of a website or application that users see and interact with. Front-End Developers are responsible for creating layouts, buttons, and pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while making sure the interface is responsive, accessible, and works smoothly across different devices.
